This is Sleep

The monophasic sleep schedule is the most common schedule in the world. It consists of sleeping in one elongated session. Polyphasic sleep means sleeping in multiple sessions throughout the day, and can save time and cut tiredness. We take the knowledge and power of this type of sleep to bring you a way to incorporate it into your life through a nimble scheduler.

The benefits of polyphasic sleep include: Saving time Feeling more awake Getting more restful sleep in a smaller period of time

Biphasic: Segmented Sleep

Segmented sleep is considered the most natural sleeping pattern. It consists of two sleeps, both at night time. For more info, click here.


Biphasic: Siesta Sleep

The siesta schedule consists of 5-6 hours of sleep at night and a 20 to 90 minute nap in the early afternoon. This form of sleep is commonly known by scientists to be healthier than monophasic sleep. For more info, click here.


Triphasic Sleep

Triphasic is an efficient and simple schedule. There is little adaptation involved in a change from monophasic sleep to this schedule. A Triphasic sleeper typically sleeps between 4 and 5 hours a day. For more info, click here.


Everyman Sleep

The Everyman schedule is the most successful reduced-sleep schedule to date. Everyman schedules have a significantly more difficult adaptation period than all of the previous schedules. For more info, click here.


Dual Core Sleep

Dual Core sleep is a derivative of the other schedules. Dual core schedules can have the benefits of both segmented sleep and siestas and so is theoretically very healthy. For more info, click here.


Uberman Sleep

Uberman is the most commonly attempted, and most failed of polyphasic schedules. This is largely due to a misunderstanding of the difficulties associated with its adaptation period. Uberman is the most well known nap only schedule. For more info, click here.


Dymaxion Sleep

Dymaxion is another popularly attempted schedule. The Dymaxion schedule involves sleeping 4 times a day for 30 minutes. For more info, click here.


SPAMAYL Sleep

SPAMAYL is the younger, fresher cousin of Uberman. SPAMAYL stands for Sleep Polyphasically As Much As You Like. SPAMAYL is more flexible than Uberman. For more info, click here.
